About the Game

Sort the Court! puts you on the throne with a single rule: answer every petition with a yes or a no. Each choice shapes your kingdom, from bustling markets to spooky visitors in the night.

The simplicity is the magic. Quick prompts, surprising outcomes, and a steady rhythm of choices make it perfect for short play sessions that still feel meaningful.
